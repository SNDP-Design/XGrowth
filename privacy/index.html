Privacy Policy

How GrowOS collects, uses, and protects your information.

Effective date: May 8, 2026 · Last updated: May 8, 2026

Quick summary
  • We use Google Sign-In to identify you. We receive your name, email, and profile picture.
  • Your dashboard data (notes, posts, campaigns, metrics) is stored locally in your browser. We do not upload it to our servers.
  • We do not sell your data. We do not show third-party ads.
  • You can delete your data anytime by clearing your browser's local storage or revoking access in your Google Account.

1. Who we are

GrowOS (the "Service") is operated by SNDP-Design ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use the Service at https://www.xgrowth.uno.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you give us via Google Sign-In

When you sign in with Google, Google shares the following information with us:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your Google profile picture URL
  • A unique Google account identifier ("sub")

We do not request access to your Gmail, Drive, Calendar, Contacts, or any other Google service. The only scopes we request are openid, email, and profile.

2.2 Information you create in the app

When you use the dashboard, content engine, campaign builder, or analytics features, the inputs and outputs (your niche, posts, campaigns, metrics, generated copy, etc.) are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly export it (Copy, Download, or Print actions).

2.3 Information collected automatically

The Service is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log standard server access information (IP address, browser type, request timestamp) for security and abuse prevention. We do not have access to or store these logs ourselves. See GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

We do not currently use cookies, analytics tools (Google Analytics, Plausible, etc.), or tracking pixels. If we add them in the future, we will update this policy.

3. How we use information

  • To authenticate you and display your name and avatar in the app.
  • To associate locally-stored dashboard data with your account on this device.
  • To respond to support requests sent to our contact email.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or to train machine-learning models.

4. How we share information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share information only in these limited cases:

  • Google: When you sign in, your authentication request goes through Google. Their handling of that data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub: Hosts the static files that make up this site. Their privacy practices are linked above.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users or the public.

5. Data retention

Information stored in your browser's local storage remains until you clear it. Data sent to us via Google Sign-In is retained only for the duration of your session in the browser; we do not maintain a server-side database.

If we add a backend in the future, we will update this section before the change takes effect.

6. Your choices and rights

  • Sign out: Click "Sign out" in the sidebar to end your session.
  • Revoke Google access: Visit myaccount.google.com/permissions to revoke this app's access to your Google Account.
  • Delete local data: Click "Reset" in the dashboard, or clear your browser's site data for www.xgrowth.uno.
  • GDPR / CCPA / state privacy rights: If you are a resident of the EU, UK, California, or another jurisdiction with privacy rights legislation, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. Email us at the address below to exercise these rights. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We rely on Google's authentication infrastructure and HTTPS encryption (provided by GitHub Pages via Let's Encrypt) to protect data in transit. Data stored in your browser is protected by your browser's standard security model. We cannot guarantee absolute security; please use a strong password on your Google Account and enable 2-step verification.

8. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. International users

The Service is operated from the United States. If you access it from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to, processed, and stored in the U.S. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change. Material changes will be communicated via the app or by email to signed-in users.

11. Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints about this policy or your data:

  • Email: support@xgrowth.uno
  • Site: https://www.xgrowth.uno
