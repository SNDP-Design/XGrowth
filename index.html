Founder questions, answered.

Is this just ChatGPT with a prompt?

No. Every output is structured for X specifically — hook formats that actually convert, thread architectures based on what 50k+ tweets show works, and templates trained on the patterns of founder accounts at YC, Tribe Capital, and a16z's portfolio. ChatGPT will give you generic content. XGrowth gives you content shaped for the platform and your stage.

Will my data be safe?

Yes. We only store: your name, email, profile picture (from Google Sign-In), and the data you create in the app. We do not access your Gmail, Drive, or Twitter account. We do not sell data, ever. Read our Privacy Policy for the full details.

Do I need to connect my Twitter / X account?

No. xgrowth.uno is a content + analytics layer that lives alongside your X account. You generate content here, paste it into X yourself, then log your top posts back into the analytics dashboard. We're not asking for your X password or API access.

What if I cancel?

Cancel anytime from the dashboard. You keep access until the end of your current billing period. Your data stays — you can re-subscribe later and pick up where you left off. No "delete my account" maze.

Is this only for SaaS / AI founders?

XGrowth is tuned for US tech founders (SaaS, AI, dev tools, e-commerce) but founders in fintech, climate, healthtech, and consumer have used it successfully. The core content frameworks are niche-agnostic; you tell us your niche during onboarding and outputs adapt.

How is this different from Buffer / Hypefury / Tweet Hunter?

Those are scheduling + analytics tools. They assume you've already written the content. We're the layer before — we help you generate the content in the first place, with founder-specific frameworks (niche statement, investor-targeted threads, launch campaigns, etc.) that scheduling tools don't touch. Pair us with one of them if you like; we don't compete.

I don't have a niche yet. Can I still use this?

Yes — that's actually a great use case. Run through Personal Brand Kit with a vague niche and 3-5 angles you might pursue. The output gives you a content calendar to test which angle gets traction. After 2 weeks you'll know which to commit to.
